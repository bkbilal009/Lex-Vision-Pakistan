## 🏛️ Project Vision
Pakistan ka legal system bohot vast hai. **Lex-Vision-Pakistan** ko **Muhammad Bilal** ne isliye design kiya taake lawyers aur students constitutional articles aur case law precedents seconds mein dhoond sakain. Ye system keyword search ke bajaye "Semantic Meaning" par kaam karta hai.

---

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture
### 1. Semantic Retrieval
* **Vectorization:** `all-MiniLM-L6-v2` ke zariye legal text ko high-dimensional embeddings mein convert karna.
* **Storage:** `ChromaDB` for high-speed similarity search across legal chunks.

### 2. Contextual Logic
* **Gemini 2.5 Flash:** Bari legal context windows ko process karne ke liye Google ka latest reasoning engine.
* **History-Aware Chain:** AI remembers previous legal inquiries for deeper multi-turn research.
